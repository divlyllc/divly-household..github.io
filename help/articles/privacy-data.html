Privacy & Your Data

Your home life is personal. Divly is built to keep your data private to your household and under your control.

Stored securely

Your data is stored securely, and your sign-in is kept in your device's secure storage — the protected area your phone reserves for sensitive things like passwords and keys.

Private to your household

Everything you create — tasks, habits, lists, check-ins — is private to your household. Only you and your partner can see it.

Apple's Hide My Email

If you signed in with Apple and chose Hide My Email, Divly only ever sees a private relay address — your real email stays hidden.

You by name, not by relay. Even when Hide My Email is on, Divly shows your name throughout the app rather than the long relay address, so things stay friendly and readable.

Removing your data

You can remove your data at any time by deleting your account from your profile. There's a 30-day grace period during which you can recover your account by signing back in; after that, your data is permanently removed.

Your choice, your control. Deleting your account is the single action that clears your data — no hidden copies left behind once the grace period ends.
