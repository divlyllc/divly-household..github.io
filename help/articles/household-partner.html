Household & Partner

Divly is built for two. A household is shared between you and your partner, so everything stays in sync and you're never out of the loop.

One shared household

A household connects the two of you. Tasks, habits, lists and your progress all live in this shared space — what one of you adds, the other sees.

Inviting your partner

  1. Share the invite link. Send your partner the household invite link from Divly.
  2. They install Divly and tap the link. That joins them to your household.
  3. You're connected. Once they've joined, the invite disappears — there's nothing left to manage.

Everything syncs in real time

Add a task on your phone and it appears on your partner's instantly. Check something off and they see it update right away. There's no refresh button — Divly keeps both phones in step automatically.

Sharing the load

You can assign any task or habit to either person, so it's always clear who's got what. Use the person filter to focus on just your items, just your partner's, or everyone together.

Fairer by design. Assigning and filtering by person makes the invisible work visible — and easier to share evenly.

Seeing each other's mood

Through the Weekly Check-in, you can see each other's mood for the week — Light, About right, or Heavy. It's a gentle way to know when your partner could use a hand.
