Getting Started with Divly

Divly helps the people in your home share the mental load — tasks, habits and lists in one place, for both of you. Here's how to get up and running in a few minutes.

Create your account

Open Divly and choose how you'd like to sign in. You can use any of:

  • Sign in with Apple — fastest on iPhone. You can choose to share or hide your email.
  • Continue with Google — uses your Google account.
  • Email & password — sign up with your email address and a password you choose.
New to Divly? Every new household starts with a 7-day free trial of Divly Premium — no payment needed to begin. See Account & Billing for what's included.

Set up your household

After signing in for the first time, the setup wizard walks you through a few quick steps:

  1. Name your household — for example "The Adventure Crew". This is the shared space you and your partner share.
  2. Tell us a little about your home — household type and a short questionnaire that helps Divly tailor suggestions to your routine. You can change your answers later.
  3. Add a few starter items — optionally pick some common tasks, habits or lists to seed your home so it isn't empty on day one.
Tip The starter items you add during setup won't count toward your achievements — they're just there to help you find your feet. Real progress starts when you complete things yourself.

Invite your partner

Divly is built for two. To share your home with your partner:

  1. Go to Profile and find the invite option in your household section.
  2. Tap to share an invite link. Send it however you like — WhatsApp, Messages, email.
  3. When your partner opens the link on their phone, it takes them straight into joining your household. If they don't have Divly yet, it points them to download it first.

Once your partner joins, everything you both add stays in sync in real time — complete a task on your phone and it updates on theirs instantly.

Households hold two people. Divly is designed for a household of two. Once your partner has joined, the invite option disappears.

Find your way around

There are three main areas, reachable from the bottom of the screen:

  • Home — your dashboard: progress rings, your Divly Score, your streak, what to focus on next, and your lists at a glance.
  • Plan — the full view of your Tasks, Habits and Lists, with filters by person and time period.
  • Ask Divly — your assistant for suggestions and planning.
  • The + button in the middle adds a new task, habit or list from anywhere.
