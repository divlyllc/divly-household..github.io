Your Home Dashboard

Home is the calm overview of your household — how today is going, what's next, and how you and your partner are doing together.

The progress rings

At the top of Home you'll see two nested rings that fill as you complete things:

  • Tasks — shown in blue.
  • Habits — shown in teal.

Tap the rings to open a drill-down. There you can switch between Tasks and Habits and see exactly what's been completed.

Tip The big ring opens to Tasks by default — use the toggle at the top of the drill-down to flip to Habits.

Your Divly Score

In the center of the rings is your Divly Score — the average of your Tasks and Habits completion. It's shown as a simple band so you can read it at a glance:

  • Strong
  • Steady
  • Low
No score yet? The score stays hidden until you have some activity, so a brand-new household never sees a discouraging zero.

Your streak

The flame shows your household streak — the number of consecutive days where someone completed a task or logged a habit. Keeping the flame alive is a shared effort: either of you can keep it going.

The Your Focus card

The Your Focus card surfaces the next thing worth doing, so you don't have to go hunting for it. From the card you can:

  • Snooze it — for tomorrow, in 3 days, or next week.
  • Reschedule or Reassign a past-due item to get it back on track.

Weekly Check-in

The Weekly Check-in lets you share how your week feels — Light, About right, or Heavy. You'll also see your partner's mood, so you can support each other when things get busy.

Your day at a glance

  • Today — everything due today, in one place.
  • Coming up — a look ahead at what's next.
  • Carried over — anything past-due that still needs attention.
  • Lists — a quick view of your shared lists.

Changing the time range

Use the time-range dropdown to switch the view between Today, This week, and This month. The rings and score update to match the range you choose.
