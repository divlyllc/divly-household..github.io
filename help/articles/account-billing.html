Account & Billing

Everything about your Divly account — your trial, your subscription, your profile, and how to leave if you ever need to.

Your free trial

Every new household gets a 7-day free trial so you and your partner can settle in and feel the difference before deciding anything.

Divly Premium

Divly Premium is a subscription that unlocks the full experience. You subscribe through the App Store iPhone or Google Play Android, so it's billed safely through your existing app store account.

One subscription per household. Billing is managed by the household owner, and Premium covers your shared household.

Restoring a purchase

Got a new phone, or reinstalled the app? Use Restore purchase to bring your subscription back. As long as you're signed in to the same app store account you bought it with, your Premium is restored.

Your profile

  • Profile photo — upload a photo so your partner sees who's who.
  • Appearance — switch between Light, Dark, or System to match your phone.

Deleting your account

If you decide to leave, you can delete your account from your profile. It's removed after a 30-day grace period.

Changed your mind? During the 30-day grace period your account can be recovered simply by signing back in. After that, it's permanently removed.
