Notifications

Stay nudged about the things that matter — and stay quiet about the things that don't. Divly lets you tune every notification to suit your household.

Where to manage them

Open Profile → Notification preferences. Everything below lives there.

Turn each type on or off

You can switch each notification type on or off individually, including:

  • Task reminders
  • When a progress ring closes
  • Carried-over tasks
  • Weekly check-in prompts
  • Habit suggestions from your partner
  • Achievements you unlock
  • …and more

Quiet hours

Set a start and end time for quiet hours so notifications hold off while you sleep or unwind. Overnight ranges work — for example, 10:00 pm to 7:00 am.

Push and email

For each type you can choose to receive it as a Push notification, an Email, or both. Mix and match so urgent nudges buzz your phone while gentler updates land in your inbox.

Allow notifications on your phone

One thing to check. Make sure notifications are allowed for Divly at the system level on your phone. If they're switched off in your device settings, no push will arrive no matter your in-app preferences.

A note on reminders

You'll only get a reminder for an item that has a reminder set. If a task isn't nudging you, open it and add a reminder.
