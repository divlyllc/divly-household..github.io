Ask Divly

Ask Divly is your built-in household assistant. It knows your household's context — your tasks, habits and routines — so it can help you plan, brainstorm and stay on top of things.

What Ask Divly can do

Think of Ask Divly as a helpful partner who's already up to speed on your home. Ask it to plan your week, suggest a routine, untangle a busy weekend, or just talk through what's on your plate.

Personalized welcome suggestions

When you open Ask Divly, you'll see a few personalized suggestions tailored to your household — a quick way to start without typing. Tap one to dive straight in.

Chatting

Type a message and Ask Divly replies in the chat, just like a conversation. Keep going back and forth to refine a plan or explore ideas together.

Action cards

When Ask Divly suggests something concrete — a task, a habit or a shopping item — it appears as an action card right in the chat. From the card you can:

  • Add — pop the suggestion straight into your tasks, habits or list.
  • Skip — dismiss it if it's not for you.
No copy-pasting. Action cards let you turn a good idea into a real task or list item in a single tap.

Your daily reply allowance

Each household gets a set number of full-quality replies per day — around 30. After that, Ask Divly keeps working on a faster model for the rest of the day, then resets the next day.

Check what's left. You can see how many full-quality replies your household has remaining today in your Profile.

Tips for great prompts

  • Be specific. "Plan a calm Sunday reset" beats "help me."
  • Mention dates and people. Naming who's involved and when helps Ask Divly tailor its suggestions.
  • Say what you want out of it — a list, a routine, or just ideas.
