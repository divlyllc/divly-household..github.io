Calendar Sync

Connect your calendar and Divly keeps your real-world commitments alongside your tasks and habits — no double entry.

Connecting a calendar

  1. Open Profile → Connections. This is where your calendars live.
  2. Choose your calendar. Connect Google Calendar or Apple / iCloud Calendar.
  3. Approve access. Follow the prompts, and your events begin mirroring into Divly automatically.

iPhone Your Apple / iCloud calendar syncs right on your device. Android and everyone can connect Google Calendar.

Turning events into tasks and habits

Divly can read your events and helpfully turn them into items for you:

  • One-time events become tasks.
  • Repeating events become habits.

Anything created this way carries an "Imported from Calendar" label so you always know where it came from.

Per-connection options

Each connected calendar has its own settings, so you stay in control:

  • Importing on/off — choose whether events from this calendar become tasks and habits.
  • Only events you organize — skip events you were merely invited to, and import just the ones you own.

Staying in sync

Connected calendars re-sync automatically — about every 15 minutes while the app is open — so changes flow through without you lifting a finger.

Want a fresh sync now? Just open the app. Opening Divly nudges your connected calendars to check for updates.

Disconnecting

To stop syncing a calendar, go back to Profile → Connections and disconnect it. Your existing items stay; only future syncing stops.
