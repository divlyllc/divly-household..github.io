Score, Streaks & Badges

A little gentle motivation. Divly turns your everyday wins into a score, a streak, and badges — so keeping your home running feels good, not nagging.

Your Divly Score

Your Divly Score is the average of your Tasks and Habits completion. It's shown with a friendly band rather than fussing over a single number:

  • Strong — 70 and above
  • Steady — 40 to 69
  • Low — under 40

Score History

Want the bigger picture? Score History shows a 12-week trend plus a monthly calendar of your daily rings, so you can see your rhythm over time.

Streaks

Your streak counts the consecutive days with at least one task completed or habit logged. It's a shared streak — either you or your partner can keep it alive.

Streak freeze

Life happens. The streak freeze lets you pause your streak for a day, a week, or a month — perfect for a holiday — so a quiet day doesn't break the run you've built.

Going away? Set a freeze before you leave and come back to your streak right where you left it.

Badges

Badges are achievements you earn for milestones along the way. When you unlock one, you'll get a little celebration, and you can revisit all of them in your badge gallery.

Earned by the doer. A badge is earned by the person who actually did the action — so your wins are yours.
