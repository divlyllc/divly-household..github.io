Tasks

Tasks are the to-dos you share as a household — anything from "book the dentist" to "take out the bins." Here's how to add, complete and tidy them up.

Adding a task

  1. Tap the + button. Choose Task from the add sheet.
  2. Give it a title. A short, clear name works best.
  3. Fill in the details. Set any of the fields below, then save.

The task fields

  • Title — what needs doing.
  • Due date — when it's due. You can't pick a date in the past.
  • Time — set it to the minute if timing matters.
  • Priority — High shows a filled pink flag, Normal shows an outline flag, and Low shows none.
  • Assigned to — choose who's responsible: you or your partner.
  • Repeat — make it recurring if it happens regularly.
  • Reminder — get a nudge before it's due.
Reminders need to be set. You'll only get a reminder for a task if you add one when creating or editing it.

Completing a task

Tap the circle next to a task to mark it done — you'll get a little celebration as it fills in. Completed tasks slide into your Completed section.

Recurring tasks

When you complete a recurring task, Divly automatically creates the next occurrence for you, carrying its details forward. There's nothing to set up again — it just reappears on its next due date.

Editing a task

Tap a task to open it, then update any field — title, date, time, priority, assignee, recurrence or reminder. As with adding, you can't move the due date into the past.

Archiving completed tasks

Once a task is done, you can archive it to clear it from your list and keep things tidy.

Your progress is always safe. Archiving a completed task hides it from view but never removes it from your records — it keeps counting toward your Divly Score and your streak. Archiving never affects your progress.
