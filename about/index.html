About Divly

We build for the household, not the individual

Divly's mission is to reduce the mental load of running a home — and to keep the household workload fair and visible between partners.

What is Divly?

Divly is a household-coordination app for couples and families. It combines shared tasks, habits and lists with real-time sync between partners, daily progress rings and a Divly Score, and Ask Divly — an AI helper that turns a request like “plan the weekend” into ready-to-add items. It is sold per household, with a 7-day free trial, on iOS and Android.

The problem: the mental load

Running a home is a second job that mostly happens in one person's head. Noticing the fridge is empty, remembering the school form, planning the weekend, tracking whose turn it is — this "mental load" is real work, and in most households it lands unevenly on one partner.

Productivity apps never solved this because they're built for individuals: your list, your reminders, your account. The household work stayed invisible, and the partner who managed it stayed the manager.

Our answer

Divly moves the household out of one partner's head and into one shared system both partners see. Shared tasks, habits and lists mean the noticing is shared. Rings and the Divly Score mean the doing is visible. Ask Divly means the planning — the heaviest part of the load — gets drafted for you.

The result we care about is not more productivity. It's fewer chore arguments, fewer dropped balls, and more evenings that feel like living together instead of managing together. Less managing. More living.

How we make money

Divly is sold per household with a 7-day free trial — that's the whole business model. We don't sell ads, and we don't sell your data. When you pay us, the incentives point one way: make your household run better.
