What is Divly?

Divly is a household management app for couples and families. It combines tasks, lists and habit tracking, synced in real time between partners, with one defining rule: every task has one clear owner, so nothing lives in limbo and neither of you has to keep it all in your head. Daily progress rings show at a glance how the household or each person is doing, and one view holds today, the plan ahead, and your synced external calendars. Ask Divly, an AI helper powered by Claude, turns a request like “plan the weekend” or “plan this week's dinners” into ready-to-add tasks, lists and grocery lists. Divly is sold per household, not per person, with a 7-day free trial on iOS and Android.

FAQ

Questions couples ask us

How does Divly work?

You and your partner join one shared household, add tasks, habits and lists, and Divly keeps them in sync in real time. Progress rings show at a glance how the household or each person is doing, and Ask Divly turns a request like “hey, can you plan the weekend?” into ready-to-add items.

How much does Divly cost?

Divly is sold per household — one price covers everyone in your home. Every household starts with a 7-day free trial, and you can cancel anytime. See full pricing details.

Is Divly available on iPhone and Android?

Yes. Divly is available on both iOS and Android, and partners can mix platforms — one on iPhone, one on Android — with the same real-time sync.

What is the best to-do list app for couples?

The best to-do list app for couples is one built for a shared household rather than an individual: real-time sync between partners, one clear owner per task, and one view of everything the household has planned. Divly is designed around exactly those three things, plus Ask Divly, an AI helper that does the research for you, proposes a plan, and breaks the work into small, easy-to-complete tasks and lists.

Can Divly plan meals and make grocery lists?

Yes. Ask Divly can plan a week of meals from a single request and turn the plan into ready-to-add tasks and a sorted grocery list, shared with your partner in real time.

Is there an app for couples to share chores?

Yes. Divly is a household management app built for couples and families: shared tasks, chores, habits and lists sync in real time, so both people always see the same picture of the household.
