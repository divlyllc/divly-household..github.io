What is Divly?

Divly is a household-coordination app for couples and families. It combines shared tasks, habits and lists with real-time sync between partners, daily progress rings and a Divly Score, and Ask Divly — an AI helper that turns a request like “plan the weekend” into ready-to-add items. It is sold per household, with a 7-day free trial, on iOS and Android.

FAQ

Questions couples ask us

Is there an app for couples to share chores?

Yes. Divly is a household management app built for couples and families: shared tasks, habits and lists sync in real time between partners, so both people see the same household in their pocket.

How does Divly work?

You and your partner join one shared household, add tasks, habits and lists, and Divly keeps them in sync in real time. Daily progress rings and a Divly Score show how the household is doing, and Ask Divly turns a request like “plan the weekend” into ready-to-add items.

How much does Divly cost?

Divly is sold per household — one price covers everyone in your home. Every household starts with a 7-day free trial, and you can cancel anytime. See full pricing details.

Is Divly available on iPhone and Android?

Yes. Divly is available on both iOS and Android, and partners can mix platforms — one on iPhone, one on Android — with the same real-time sync.
