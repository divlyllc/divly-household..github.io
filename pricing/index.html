Billing FAQ

Pricing questions, answered directly

Does Divly have a free trial?

Yes. Every Divly household starts with a 7-day free trial with full access to all features. You can cancel anytime during the trial and pay nothing.

Is Divly priced per person or per household?

Per household. One Divly plan covers everyone in your home — you never pay per seat, and adding your partner costs nothing extra.

How do I cancel my Divly subscription?

You can cancel anytime from the app's settings or through your App Store or Google Play subscription settings. Your household keeps full access until the end of the paid period.

What happens to our data if we cancel?

Your household's tasks, habits and lists stay readable, and you can export or delete your data at any time. Write access resumes if you resubscribe. Questions? support@divly.co.
