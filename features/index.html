Features

The shared to-do list & chore app built for your household

Divly gives the two of you one shared system for the whole household: to-do lists, tasks and habits that sync in real time, where every task has one clear owner, with rings for your day at a glance and an AI helper that does the thinking for you.

Shared tasks, habits & lists

Every item in Divly is shared with your household and syncs in real time between partners. There's no "my list" and "your list" to reconcile. Just one household you both see.

  • Tasks surface what needs doing today: errands, repairs, calls to make.
  • Habits protect the small daily things that keep you at your best: laundry day, plant watering, meal prep.
  • Lists are saved for when a list actually makes sense: groceries, packing, gifts.

You need all three to run a home well, and Divly is the one app that gives your household all three in one view.

app screenshot: shared tasks list, two assignees

Every task has one owner

Divly won't let a task exist without an owner. Not a shared pile both of you glance at and assume the other has covered: one name on every task, from the moment it's created.

  • Assign to yourself or your partner as you create, every time.
  • Reassigning is one tap, and it hands over the whole job, not just the reminder.
  • When something pops into your head, check Divly, see it's owned, and let it go.

Shared lists create ambiguity. Ownership creates trust. This one rule is why nothing falls between two people who each thought the other had it.

app screenshot: task creation with owner selection

Progress rings & your day at a glance

Progress rings give you a quick read on how things are going, and one view holds your whole day. Today's tasks and habits, the plan ahead, and your connected calendars, together.

  • Rings for the household, or filtered to each person.
  • See everything on today's plate, or look ahead at the full plan.
  • Your calendar events sit alongside your household's tasks, so nothing gets planned on top of anything.
app screenshot: rings + today view with calendar events

Streaks & badges

Closing your rings builds streaks, and milestones unlock badges, so keeping the home running feels like wins your household can celebrate.

  • A personal streak for closing your own rings.
  • A household streak you can only keep alive together.
  • One busy day doesn't erase your progress. Streaks in Divly are built to encourage, never to punish.
app screenshot: streaks + badge gallery

Weekly check-ins

Some weeks hit one of you harder than the other. Divly's check-in is how the load shifts. A quick weekly moment where you both see how the week looks, and whoever has more room takes something off the other's plate.

  • See the week ahead together in one view.
  • Hand tasks over in a tap when one of you is stretched thin.
  • A shared system that adjusts to real life, never a scoreboard.
app screenshot: weekly check-in screen

Ask Divly, your AI household helper

Ask Divly, powered by Claude, does the thinking for your household. Just ask, the way you'd text a friend: it does the research, proposes a plan, and builds the tasks, habits and lists. You approve before anything is added.

  • "hey, can you plan dinners this week?" comes back as two lists: the week's meal plan and an organized grocery list.
  • "set up a weekly cleaning routine for us" comes back as habits with owners, ready to add.
  • "we leave Friday, get us ready" comes back as packing lists and prep tasks, each with a name on it.

Connect your calendars

Connect your calendars and Divly plans around real life. Your existing events show up alongside the household's tasks and habits, so the plan respects what's already scheduled.

  • Ask Divly plans around your existing events.
  • See busy days at a glance before assigning tasks.
  • Optional, and you can disconnect anytime.

FAQ

Feature questions, answered directly

Is Divly a shared to-do list app?

Yes, and more than one. Divly is a shared to-do list, task, and habit app built for a two-adult household: everything syncs in real time between partners, every task has one clear owner, and lists live alongside your to-dos and habits in one view.

Can both partners edit the same lists?

Yes. Every task, habit and list in a Divly household is shared, and changes sync in real time between partners on iOS and Android.

Does Divly have reminders?

Yes. Divly sends reminders to keep the day on track, and Ask Divly adds what reminders can't: it thinks ahead, proposing plans and building tasks before the week catches you off guard.

Does Ask Divly need access to my calendar?

No. Calendar connect is optional. If you connect it, Divly shows your events alongside your household's plan and Ask Divly plans around them, and you can disconnect anytime.

What's a good alternative to a shared to-do list?

A household app like Divly. Beyond a shared to-do list, Divly adds one clear owner per task, habits with streaks, daily progress rings, weekly check-ins, and an AI helper that researches and drafts your plans for you.
