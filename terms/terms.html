What Divly is

Divly is a household coordination app for couples and families: shared tasks, habits, lists, progress tracking, and an AI helper. It's a planning tool — AI suggestions are ideas to consider, not medical, financial, legal, or other professional advice.

Your account

Keep your sign-in to yourself and tell us if you suspect misuse. The household owner manages billing, member removal, and account deletion for the whole household.

Don't abuse the service: no attempts to break security, scrape other households, overload the AI features, or use Divly for anything unlawful.
